Usage Instructions

This app is designed to help you access drug information easily. Before using any medication, always make sure you follow your doctor's instructions.

All information provided by this app is for informational purposes only and does not replace medical advice. If you have any questions about your medication, please consult your doctor.

If you have any issues or concerns about your medication, you can ask the chatbot for data and dosage information.

Follow these basic steps when using your medications:

  • Follow your doctor's instructions carefully.
  • If you have questions about dosage, ask your doctor.
  • Consult your doctor about allergens and drug interactions.
  • Do not stop your medication early.
  • Store your medication properly and check expiration dates.

Proper use of medications is important for your health. You can easily access all drug data and dosage information through the app.
